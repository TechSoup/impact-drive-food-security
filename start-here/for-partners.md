# For funders and partners
The Impact Drive is TechSoup's open knowledge base for civil society
technology — sector by sector, starting with food security, with support
from Esri.
## What a founding partner relationship looks like
We are in a founding partner phase. Founding partners:
- Contribute their nonprofit offer to the solutions library
- Are named in the repo and in TechSoup's public communications as a
  founding partner of the Impact Drive program
- Help shape the product before it goes to broad market
## How to engage
Reach out to [name / email] or open an issue in this repo describing
your interest. We are looking for the organizations that want to help
build what this becomes — not selling off the shelf yet.
