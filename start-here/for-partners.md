# For funders and partners
The Impact Drive is TechSoup's open knowledge base for civil society
technology. Starting with food security the Impact Drive will continue to expand with new sector knowledge over time. The Food Security Impact Drive was made possible with support from Esri.
## What a founding partner relationship looks like
We are in a founding partner phase. Founding partners:
- Contribute their nonprofit design to the solutions library
- Are named in the repo and in TechSoup's public communications as a
  founding partner of the Impact Drive program during early stage development (first 60 days of the program)
- Help shape the product before it goes to broad market

## How to engage
[Reach out](https://page.techsoup.org/impact-drive#getinvolved) or open an issue in this repo describing
your interest. We are looking for organizations that want to help
build what this Impact Drive becomes.
