# For CSR and corporate giving teams

The food security Impact Drive gives your team a validated, ready-to-use
picture of the organizations you want to reach and the problems they are
actually trying to solve. Deep community engagement and community research by TechSoup brought you this information, not vendor data.

## What you can do with this

- Use the personas and jobs-to-be-done to sharpen how you describe your
program's impact to leadership and board members
- Use the use cases to identify where your technology or funding creates
the most leverage
- Use the sector landscape to brief internal stakeholders on the organizations, the people working in the space,
and why food security technology is hard
- Bring what you have learned to your nonprofit partners as a shared starting point to
co-design solutions together

## What a founding partner relationship looks like
We are in a founding partner phase. Founding partners:
- Contribute their nonprofit design to the solutions library
- Help shape the product before it goes to broad market
- Are named in the repo and in TechSoup's public communications as a
  founding partner of the Impact Drive program during early stage development (first 60 days of the program)


## How to engage

If you want to add what you designed to the solutions library, [get in touch](https://page.techsoup.org/impact-drive#getinvolved) and tell us more about it.
