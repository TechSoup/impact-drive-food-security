# For CSR and corporate giving teams
The food security Impact Drive gives your team a validated, ready-to-use
picture of the organizations you want to reach and the problems they are
actually trying to solve — built by TechSoup from direct community research,
not vendor data.
## What you can do with this
- Use the personas and jobs-to-be-done to sharpen how you describe your
  program's impact to leadership and board
- Use the use cases to identify where your technology or funding creates
  the most leverage
- Use the sector landscape to brief internal stakeholders on who the
  players are and why food security technology is hard
- Bring it to your nonprofit partners as a shared starting point for
  co-design
## How to engage
If you want your offer — what's available to food security nonprofits,
at what price, with what support — listed in the solutions library,
open an issue or reach out directly to [name / email].
Founding partners who contribute their offer are named in the repo and
in TechSoup's public communications about the Impact Drive program.
