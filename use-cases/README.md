# Use Cases: the buildable units

Each use case is a discrete user story a developer can implement, linked up to a parent
[Job to be Done](../jobs-to-be-done/) and a [persona](../personas/). A builder can pull one without taking
the whole job.

### Client-side: Find convenient, dignified food support
- [Find nearby pantries by hours, location, and selection](find-nearby-pantries.md)
- [Set food preferences and request items in an anonymous profile](client-anonymous-profile.md)
- [Report anonymously what food was eaten and what wasn't](anonymous-food-feedback.md)

### Provider-side: Distribute all donated food each session
- [Update pantry hours and inventory from a simple dashboard](provider-update-pantry.md)
- [See and stock items the community requested](community-requested-items.md)
- [Connect a disconnected pantry to the regional network](connect-to-network.md)

### Provider-side: Distribute the right food to the right pantries before it spoils
- [View aggregated pantry inventory and consumption without PII](aggregated-pantry-data.md)
- [Discover newly-joined and nearby pantries on a regional map](discover-pantries-on-map.md)
- [Match warehouse stock to pantry capacity and demonstrated need](match-stock-to-need.md)
