## User story
> As a volunteer pantry coordinator, I want to see what items visitors are requesting, so I can ask our
> congregation for those things and stock what the community actually wants.

## What a solution might do
Surface aggregated client requests to the pantry, turn them into a shareable "what folks need" list for an
engaged congregation, and let the coordinator confirm when requested items are added. Closes the loop
between client requests (see [client-anonymous-profile](client-anonymous-profile.md)) and what's stocked.

## Evidence
- Validated by: ESRI Generate and Design — Gladys adds visitor-requested items; the congregation loves a
  list of what's needed, in [Personal Pantry](../designs/personal-pantry.md).
