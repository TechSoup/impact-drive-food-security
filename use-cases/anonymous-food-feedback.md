## User story
> As a client, I want to report anonymously what food my family ate and what we didn't (e.g. milk), so
> pantries can stock what we'll actually use, without anyone tracing it back to me.

## What a solution might do
A lightweight feedback loop, e.g. scan a QR code at pickup, then later submit what was received, eaten,
and not eaten. **All PII is deleted and not recorded.** Optionally reward engagement with an incentive
that grows over time. Aggregated, non-identifying signals feed pantries and food banks to reduce waste.

## Evidence
- Validated by: ESRI Generate and Design: the QR feedback flow and "more data on what is eaten" need in
  [One System, One World](../designs/one-system-one-world.md); pantries voiced this as a critical need.
