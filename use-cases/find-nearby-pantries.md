## User story
> As a client, I want to find nearby pantries filtered by when they're open, where they are, and what they
> stock, so I can pick one that fits my schedule, my transportation, and what my family will eat.

## What a solution might do
A searchable/mappable directory of pantries showing hours, location, offerings, payment and delivery
options. Surface less-obvious options (e.g. a pantry farther away but open in the evening) and signals
from other clients about selection and environment. Keep it light for limited-data Android phones.
