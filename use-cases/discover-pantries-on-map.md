## User story
> As a food-bank program manager, I want to be notified of new pantries in my region and see the network
> of pantries on a map, so I can connect with small, previously-invisible pantries and coordinate coverage.

## What a solution might do
A regional map of the pantry network with notifications when a new (often small, hyper-local) pantry joins.
Reveal coverage overlaps (e.g. two pantries open the same hours less than a mile apart) to prompt
conversations about offsetting hours. Enable introductions ahead of community food events.
