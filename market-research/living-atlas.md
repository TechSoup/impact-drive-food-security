# Explore the data yourself

> ArcGIS Living Atlas of the World is the foremost collection of geographic information from around the globe. It includes maps, apps, and data layers to support your work — imagery, basemaps, demographics and lifestyle, boundaries and places, and more, curated and ready to use.

Access [ArcGIS Living Atlas](https://livingatlas.arcgis.com/en/home/) to explore authoritative geographic datasets relevant to what you're building.
