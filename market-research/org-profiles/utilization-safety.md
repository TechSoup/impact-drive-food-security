# Org profiles: 3.3 Food utilization

> One of the UN's [four dimensions of food security](README.md). Baseline numbers for a proof of concept,
> subject to change.

| Attribute | Details |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Official focus** | The nutritional and biological use of food. |
| **Description** | How the body processes and absorbs nutrients. Requires food that is safe, nutritious, and properly prepared. Heavily dependent on non-food factors: clean water, adequate sanitation, and healthcare that prevent water-borne illness. Good care and feeding practices, food preparation, dietary diversity, and intra-household distribution together determine nutritional status. |
| **Total utilization orgs** | 4,618 |
| **Median budget USD (self-reported)** | $80,000 |
| **Excludes non-reporting segment** | 10.8% |
