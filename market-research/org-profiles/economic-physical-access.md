# Org profiles: 3.2 Economic & physical access to food

> One of the UN's [four dimensions of food security](README.md). Baseline numbers for a proof of concept,
> subject to change.

| Attribute | Details |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Official focus** | The economic and physical allocation of food. |
| **Description** | Having enough food nationally does not guarantee individuals can get it. Access is defined by a household's ability to acquire food: incomes, purchasing power, real food prices, and physical market infrastructure (roads, transit, proximity). It can also include traditional rights to common resources. An adequate national supply does not in itself guarantee household-level food security. |
