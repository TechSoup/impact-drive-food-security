# Organization profiles

Who are the organizations behind the [sizing data](../sizing-data.md)? These profiles describe them on the
two dimensions the market-research team produces, **budget size** (median self-reported USD) and
**mission**, organized by the **UN's four dimensions of food security**.

> **Baseline for a proof of concept: subject to change.** An organization can span more than one
> dimension, so counts across files overlap (the sizing table de-duplicates to unique orgs per region).

## The four dimensions

| # | Dimension | File | What it covers |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3.1 | **Availability** | [availability.md](availability.md) | The supply side: food physically present (production, imports, stocks, aid). |
| 3.2 | **Economic & physical access** | [economic-physical-access.md](economic-physical-access.md) | Whether people can actually get food: income, prices, market infrastructure. |
| 3.3 | **Utilization** | [utilization-safety.md](utilization-safety.md) | Nutritional and biological use: safe, nutritious, properly prepared food; WASH. |
| 3.4 | **Stability** | [stability-resilience.md](stability-resilience.md) | The temporal dimension: dependable access over time, resilience to shocks. |

These map to the four food-security categories used throughout the corpus: `availability`,
`economic-physical-access`, `utilization-safety`, and `stability-resilience`.

## How to read the profiles

- **Validation.** **North America (US)** is the most validated cohort (near-zero non-reporting segment).
  Other regions are **hypothesized**: plausible, useful to ask partners about, not yet verified.
- **Median budget** is self-reported and each region notes the **non-reporting segment excluded** from
  the median. A very low or default figure often means "no data," not "no money."
- **Featured profile** is an illustrative org archetype for the region: a concrete picture for a builder,
  not a single named organization.

See also the reference **[org-schema.md](../org-schema.md)** for a machine-readable way to describe an org.
