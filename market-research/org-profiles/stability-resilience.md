# Org profiles: 3.4 Stability

> One of the UN's [four dimensions of food security](README.md). Baseline numbers for a proof of concept,
> subject to change.

| Attribute | Details |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Official focus** | The temporal dimension: time and resilience. |
| **Description** | A population, household, or individual must have dependable access to adequate food at all times, without risk of losing it to sudden shocks (economic crises, price volatility, human-induced disasters, extreme weather) or cyclical patterns (the "lean season" before harvest). Even adequate intake today counts as insecure if access is periodically at risk. |
