## Who they are
Marguerita, 41, struggles to put food on the table. She has three kids still in school (ages 7, 10, and
13). Part of the working poor, she works two jobs and feels there is never enough time, making tough
choices between paying bills and buying food.

## Goal
Keep a roof over her head and reliably pay bills each month. Food support has to fit around that without
adding risk or hassle.

## Digital capability
Owns a cell phone; relies on public wifi and a basic plan at home when she can afford it.

## Challenges
Making hard choices with too little time. She's worried about ICE and keeping her family safe, which
raises the stakes on privacy and anonymity in any service she uses.
