## Who they are
Reba, 35, is a single parent of three. Between her job and raising her kids she's stretched thin, and she
needs assistance feeding her family. She is a regular user of a community pantry but is open to other
resources that better fit her family's changing needs.

## Goal
Anonymity, convenience, and timing: get the food her family needs without judgment, on a schedule that
fits her life.

## Digital capability
Uses an Android phone with limited mobile data.

## Challenges
Transportation, time, and information access. Finding which pantries are open when, and what they offer,
is hard with the time and data she has.
