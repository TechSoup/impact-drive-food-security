## Who they are
Dan, 45, is a Program Manager at a food bank. He's responsible for getting food out of the warehouses and
to the pantries, but he doesn't know much about the individual pantries or the specific needs of the
people who visit them, and he doesn't yet think deeply about food waste or specific needs.

## Goal
Move food out of the warehouse before it begins to spoil, to the right places.

## Digital capability
Full access to a computer, the latest iPhone, and high-speed internet. Digitally savvy and curious.

## Challenges
Dan doesn't know what to send to each pantry, and he doesn't know the current capacity of each pantry.
Small, disconnected pantries are largely invisible to him.
