## Who they are
Gladys, 75, is a volunteer at a city church that opens a food pantry twice a month, two hours at a time,
to anyone who needs it. She is representative of the many small, hyper-local, volunteer-led pantries that
sit outside formal networks.

## Goal
Distribute all of the food donated by the congregation at each scheduled distribution time.

## Digital capability
Owns an iPad, uses a flip phone, and has wifi at home and at the church.

## Challenges
Limited refrigeration and storage; few volunteers, most with limited capacity. The pantry isn't promoted
widely because of volunteer skills and time, and it has **no succession plan** for when current volunteers
step back.
