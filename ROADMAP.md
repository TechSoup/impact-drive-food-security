# Roadmap

Keep the initial launch **laser-focused**; don't boil the ocean. The logic: get one
creator to tangibly build *something* (whether it serves one org or thousands doesn't matter yet), then
scale. Market-size ambition comes *after* we've shown the create-a-solution loop works.
